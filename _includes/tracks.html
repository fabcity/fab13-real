Fabricating Society

Each year members of the more than 1,000 worldwide Fab Labs gather to share, discuss, collaborate and create communities around the different local and global interests regarding digital manufacturing, innovation and technology.

This year, The 13th International Fab Lab Meeting will be held in Santiago de Chile, and "Fabricating Society" will be its central topic. Over the past 20 years Chile has reached economic development indexes that classify it as a prosperous country in development. However, there are many gaps that exist in certain strategic dimensions that make the process of constructing a developed society quite challenging.
